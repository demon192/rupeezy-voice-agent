SLIDE 2 — PROBLEM
=====================================

The Problem

• Rupeezy's partner program offers 100% brokerage share to Authorized Persons
• Converting leads requires 30-40 manual calls/day per RM
• RMs can't speak all regional languages (8+ Indian languages)
• No consistency in pitch delivery or objection handling
• Manual lead scoring is subjective and slow
• Follow-ups fall through the cracks

💰 Cost: ₹15-20 per call × 40 calls/day × 250 days = ₹1.5-2L/year per RM
📉 Conversion Rate: Only 5-8% with manual calls

=====================================
SLIDE 3 — SOLUTION
=====================================

Our Solution: RupeezyVoice

An AI voice agent that:

1. Calls leads in their preferred language (8 Indian languages)
2. Follows the structured telecalling script (Appendix A)
3. Handles all 5 standard objections with contextual responses
4. Qualifies leads as Hot/Warm/Cold automatically (0-100 score)
5. Hands off to human RMs with full transcript + recommended action
6. Sends WhatsApp follow-ups with one click

🎯 Result: RMs focus ONLY on closing qualified Hot leads

SLIDE 7 — LANGUAGE DETECTION
=====================================

Intelligent Language Handling

• Script-based detection (Tamil, Telugu, Gujarati, Bengali scripts)
• Romanized word detection (200+ regional keywords mapped)
• Hinglish detection (Hindi+English code-mixing ratio)
• Auto-switch mid-conversation if lead changes language
• TTS reads agent responses in correct language

Example:
  Lead (Bengali): "Ami interested, bolo ki ache"
  → Detected: Bengali
  → Agent responds in romanized Bengali
  → TTS speaks in Bengali voice

=====================================
SLIDE 8 — LEAD SCORING
=====================================

Automatic Lead Qualification

HOT (Score 70-100):
  • Asks "how do I sign up?" or "send link"
  • Shows excitement about 100% share
  • Mentions having clients ready
  → Action: Immediate RM callback

WARM (Score 40-69):
  • Says "sounds interesting" but wants time
  • Engages 3+ minutes without committing
  → Action: WhatsApp follow-up + schedule callback

COLD (Score 0-39):
  • Says "not interested" firmly
  • Disconnects quickly
  → Action: Add to nurture sequence

Scoring factors: hot phrases (+12), questions asked, duration,
message count, cold signals (-20), objection patterns
